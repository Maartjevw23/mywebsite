I am a PhD candidate in the Applied Economics department at Erasmus University
Rotterdam. I specialize in applied microeconomics, with a particular interest in
health, labor and family economics. 

I will be on the 2026-2027 job market. 

My job market paper studies the effects of Dutch survivor benefit
reforms on widows and their children's long-run outcomes,
using a regression discontinuity design.
